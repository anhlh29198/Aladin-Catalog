Core Values

01Human Developmentbody_system

"Talent is the national resource", the company is committed to human development as the number one focus for outstanding development and success of BHAG.

02 A Family diversity_3

Aladin wishes to build a close co-worker relationship like a family, a team of people with virtue and talent to contribute to the development of the common house, and also willing to help each other at work and share problems in life.

03 Customer is Number 1 support_agent

Always put customer satisfaction as the number 1 priority in all of our thoughts and actions. Always be Proactive - Polite - Smile - Greeting - Thank you and treat customers like family.

04 Hard-Working engineering

Aladin always aims for fairness in work, everyone has the same opportunity to work, to be able to do what they love, be ready to put in the work and see things to the end and never give up.

05 Think Differently cognition

It is our individuality that make us different, the core value to be confident, to be who we are and not anyone else. An individual who knows how to take advantage of their own strength and create their own marks.

06 Teamwork groups

Each Aladiner always believes in perfect resonance, harmounious interaction between individuals to build our collective strength. We believe in the saying "The strength of the team is each individual member. The strength of each member is the team".

07 Win-Win Mindset partner_exchange

Aladin always aims for a long-term and sustainable business relationship between the collective partners and customers. Always accompanies and stands by them when needed with an attitude of listening, learning and progressing.
